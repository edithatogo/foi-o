## Asset Requirements

Every final publication asset must retain a caption, text alternative, source
inputs, generation command, provenance note, and intended location. Interactive
graph data must have a static textual or diagram fallback.

## External Gates

Journal-specific figure sizing, source-package rendering, final alt-text
format, and upload acceptance are external gates until refreshed against the
selected venue and approved by a human operator.
