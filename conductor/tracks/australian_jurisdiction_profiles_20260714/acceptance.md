archive and extraction pins; the sampling design and human roles are approved;
the sample and duplicate clusters are frozen; two independent human annotation
sets and distinct adjudication are locked; reliability and extractor metrics
are computed with all adverse results retained; and separate named-human
maturity decisions are recorded.

manifest is deliberately not ready and profile promotion is always false.
