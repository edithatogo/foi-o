# Version-control workflow

FOI-O work is preserved in the live worktree. Before edits, inspect branch,
remote identity, uncommitted changes, and coordinated repositories. Never reset,
discard, overwrite, rebase, force-push, merge, publish, release, or tag without
the applicable human gate. Local implementation, validation, issue comments,
and project wiring may proceed when they are within the declared programme
scope; external capture, legal/profile promotion, paper/dataset publication,
and release actions remain gated.

Every material change must remain traceable to a Conductor track, requirement,
issue, test or review result, and an append-only evidence or decision record.
